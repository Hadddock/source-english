Present Simple
The present simple is used to talk about habits, routines, and facts.
Subject-Verb Agreement in Present Simple
In the present simple, the verb form changes depending on the subject. For most verbs, add -s or -es to the base form when the subject is he, she, or it.

Verb
Example
I, you, we, they
base form (work, play, eat)

He, she, it

She plays tennis.
